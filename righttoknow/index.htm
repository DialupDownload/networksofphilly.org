Networks of Philly — Right to Know

What?

In order to support the networksofphilly.org project, we will be creating a series of Right To Know requests submitted to various organizations within the Philadelphia government to gain information on equipment they posses.

These efforts have been inspired by the Aaron Swartz Day Police Surveillance Project #ASDPSP.

How?

To simplify the process, we are using Muckrock, a non-profit collaborative news site, to file requests. Muckrock requires a small fee to process each request, but they have a swathe of features including easy-to-use templating, automatic follow-up, document scanning, and hosting for documents that return through inquiries.
